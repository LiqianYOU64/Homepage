    • Design Exploration

    My individual design exploration is related to the memorial tour. In spite of the two world wars, there have been countless wars in the world. Many people fought for their families and countries, but few have been remembered by young generations. So, I want to build a website which allows users to find and select memorial tours in QLD.

    After conducting the world cafe method on week 6, I found that there are fewer data and information websites on animal protection, while there are many valuable animal datasets on SLQ. Our team named animal protector was set up in week 6 and we focused on developing three main functions: news, game, information and education.

    Main Project

    This website collects data from the wildlife of QLD API with the functions of recent news related to wild animal protection, the game of learning animal protection knowledge, the animal hospitals searching, and emergency chatroom. It aims at raising the awareness of how important to protect the endangered wild animals. Besides, viewers have a chance to learn basic information about endangered wild animals in QLD, including their inhabitants, description, distribution and potential threats. And there is a game called super animal that helps viewers to learn more about extinction animals.

    As a designer in our team, I am responsible for the design and multimedia work in the whole project, for instance: sketch, storyboard, scenario, paper prototype, HIFI prototype, video shooting and editing, website visualization, game interface, etc. These will be presented in the following sections.

    • Catch Cash

    It is a somatosensory game called Catch Cash. Inspired by the game Daddy Pig In Avalanche, I created a character named Yuri. The player stands in front of the camera and move the body to control Yuri. When catching the colourful diamonds, the user will get related scores. The game will be terminated when catching black diamonds and the Arduino gift box will open automatically. Players can get chocolates or candies as rewards.

    • FoodCare

    FoodCare is my research project during the last year of master study. It is a smarthome based food detection system support specific dietary for those with medical conditions. There are three modes with different food detection datasets and various interactions including pregnancy, vegetarian and seniors. Users can select one mode, login Costco account and upload the online shopping list. When Parcel delivery, the food detection system is working. AI camera on the crisper can detect food categories and monitor expired date and give voice feedback to the users.
